Renewable Energy Certificates (RECs) are tradable green energy attributes that represent units of electricity generated from renewable energy resources. RECs are sold separately from commodity electricity. Corporates that wish to meet sustainability targets may choose to purchase RECs as part of their renewable energy sourcing strategy.

Originator

Companies who generate electricity or RECs and wish to sell their RECs to buyers on the marketplace.

REC Platform

Through blockchain technology, we enable fast and convenient trading of RECs based on user-defined preferences.

Buyer

Companies wanting to do their part on sustainability put in their demand to be matched automatically to sellers.

Seller

Does not need to own a generation plant, they can simply buy and sell RECs
